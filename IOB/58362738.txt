The
Abandoned
Cart
Lite
for
WooCommerce
plugin
for
WordPress
is
vulnerable
to
authentication
bypass
in
versions
up
to
,
and
including
,
5.14.2
.
This
is
due
to
insufficient
encryption
on
the
user
being
supplied
during
the
abandoned
cart
link
decode
through
the
plugin
.
This
allows
unauthenticated
attackers
to
log
in
as
users
who
have
abandoned
the
cart
,
which
users
are
typically
customers
.
