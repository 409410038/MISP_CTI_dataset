Affected
versions
of
Atlassian
Confluence
Server
allow
remote
attackers
who
have
read
permissions
to
a
page
,
but
not
write
permissions
,
to
upload
attachments
via
a
Broken
Access
Control
vulnerability
in
the
attachments
feature
.
The
affected
versions
are
before
version
7.19.9
.
This
vulnerability
was
discovered
by
Rojan
Rijal
of
the
Tinder
Security
Engineering
Team
.
