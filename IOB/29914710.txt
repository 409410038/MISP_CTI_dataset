An
attacker
exploits
a
weakness
in
the
MIME
conversion
routine
to
cause
a
buffer
overflow
and
gain
control
over
the
mail
server
machine
.
The
MIME
system
is
designed
to
allow
various
different
information
formats
to
be
interpreted
and
sent
via
e-mail
.
Attack
points
exist
when
data
are
converted
to
MIME
compatible
format
and
back
.
